⚡ How it works

Four steps. Zero hassle.

So simple your team uses it from day one — no training needed.

01

Scan the area QR

Each area has its own QR code. The employee arrives and scans it with their phone. No app to install.

02

Enter their PIN

Each employee has a personal 4-digit PIN. They enter it and the system knows who did the cleaning — no complicated logins.

03

Log the cleaning

The record is saved with the exact time and the employee's name. You can require a photo in the areas you choose.

04

Supervise in real time

You and your supervisors see all activity from the dashboard, instantly. No calls, no waiting for reports.
